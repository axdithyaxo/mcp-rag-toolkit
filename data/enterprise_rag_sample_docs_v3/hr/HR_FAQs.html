Human Resources - Frequently Asked Questions

General Employment

Q: What is the standard work week?
A: 40 hours (Mon-Fri, 9AM–5PM local time).

Q: Is remote work allowed?
A: Yes, up to 3 days per week depending on team agreements.

Leave & Holidays

Q: How is vacation approved?
A: Through the HR portal (ZenPeople), with 2-week notice.

Q: Do holidays vary by region?
A: Yes, we observe local public holidays.

Performance & Growth

Q: How often are performance reviews conducted?
A: Bi-annually — in June and December.

Q: Can I apply for internal roles?
A: Yes, after 12 months in your current role.
